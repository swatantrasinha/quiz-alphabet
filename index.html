🎓 Alphabet Quiz

A fun voice-based quiz for kids learning the alphabet!

How it works

  • 🔤 A letter appears on screen
  • 🎤 Click Start and say the letter out loud
  • ⏹ Click Stop to submit your answer
  • 🎉 Get a confetti celebration for correct answers!
  • 📊 Track your score as you go

Admin Controls

Use the Admin page to set a specific letter for the quiz, or let it pick random letters.
